OneRoot - Why, how and what?
Smallhold and marginal farmer’s contribute upto 90% share in the Indian agriculture industry and about  50% of them are debt ridden. Most of the 2nd generation farmers are moving to urban cities for a 9-5 job.

They usually cannot afford to get advice from experts nor have the exposure or network to sell directly.

“At OneRoot we make it easier for farmer’s to get advice on farm management based on satellite data of many crucial parameters such as NPK, pH, EC, moisture etc and different measures for plant health for free of cost. “
All this is good, but where do they sell their produce for fair prices that pay off for all the hard work?

OneRoot has created India’s most transparent and traceable marketplace where farmers can sell their produce directly to institutional / enterprise buyers for fair prices yielding them higher profits, transparency and giving them the pricing control for the crop they grow in their own hands instead of brokers / agents.

On the other hand, buyers get to purchase from farmers directly at reasonable prices transparently than ever before.

OneRoot is building the largest network of Smallhold and Marginal farmers in India, and through our app helping them with farm advisory, market linkage, financial assistance, government schemes and making rural life feasible again.
